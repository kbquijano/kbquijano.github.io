Unique and Cheap Personalized Supplies

September 17,
by Kate
in Product Review
3 min read

We all have different preferences in choosing school or office supplies. We also consider the price, aesthetic and quality of the product. Since people have different design perspectives, we avail personalized products but the downside is, custom-made items are NOT that cheap. What if you choose your own design and pay less?

I was searching the internet for gift ideas and mostly, the suggestion was to buy a personalized gift so that the receiver will feel more special. But, when I searched for the prices of these products, most of them are expensive even though I got my desired design, these are still beyond my budget. As I browse different online shops, there were other cheap customized items but most of them did not meet my expectations. For the price, the quality is not that good. And finally, after the endless searching, I found out about the PaperView store that sells customized office supplies. Their products looks expensive but the price are lesser than expected.

One of my favorite product in PaperView was the customized notebooks since we can choose from different sizes, colors and designs. Also, it was way to cheaper than other personalized notebooks. In my own experience, it is hard to find notebooks with plain papers because most papers are ruled. PaperView made it possible to choose what type of cover and paper I want for my notebook . The cover could be printed with pictures or names to make it more unique.

Because I like writing on plain paper because I like designing every pages, I want to create a notebook that will also be unique. PaperView offers different selections on the materials to be used for the cover, the number of pages for the notebook, orientation, and size. Also, they offer other personalized items such as pens, folders, envelopes, and more office supplies to complete the customized trend.

If you are planning to buy a special gift for your love ones and friends, I recommend the store PaperView since you will pay less for a higher quality. You can find the physical store at Uptown Mall BGC and the online store at www.paperview.com.ph

I bought these items at the online store with a total of only 200 pesos! (excluding the shipping fee). Cheap for a complete personalized collection!
