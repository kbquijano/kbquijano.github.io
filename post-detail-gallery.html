Movies Worth Binge-Watching

September 19,
by Kate
in Movies
3 min read

If I have a lot of spare time (Even if I don't have time, I still watch movies while doing other tasks XD), I watch either korean drama series, american sci-fi series, or asian and american movies. Personally, I prefer movies that are based on true stories because these makes me believe of the impossible and also inspired me in different aspects of life.

So here are some of my top favorite movies of all time where I would recommend to all of you.

5. A Quiet Place

This not so silent movie will also make you feel tensed and scared as if you are in the movie itself. When you watch this movie, you will also feel scared when someone makes noise. You unconsciously cover your mouth as if you will be heard by the 'monsters'. This is a great movie about a family that is struggling and quietly survives in the middle of the chaotic place. Warning: Always be careful of nails.

4. Theory of Everything

If you want to know more about Stephen Hawkings and his theory, watch this movie. You will also learn about the love story of Stephen and his wife and how he became what he is. This is a wonderful movie that will surely inspire people especially those who likes to explore and loves physics and sciences. You will surely like how Eddie Redmayne portray Stephen Hawkings as if he is the real Stephen Hawkings.

3. Hacksaw Ridge

If you like war movies, Hacksaw Ridge is the one for you because it is set on a war zone. It is about the lives and experiences of soldiers and how the main character saves his comrades despite every hardships that they have gone through. Watch this movie to learn about the hero of Hacksaw Ridge.

2. Our Times

If you are up to asian dramas that involves love stories, this movie is for you. This is a taiwanese drama about how the lives of students, having a crush, falling in love, and sacrificing something in the name of love. The main leads are good-looking and adorable since most of the scenes are shot in a school setting. If you want to feel young and in-love again. Plus, there's a major surprise at the end.

1. Along With The Gods

This movie will take you to a breath taking adventure in the after-life where three guardians will guide you through different trials. There will be multiple series for this movie but if you are planning to watch the first movie, be ready for some tear-jerking scenes. The whole movie will make you curious about the lead and when you found out the truth, it will make you cry and in awe. Be sure to watch the first movie first before watching the second!
