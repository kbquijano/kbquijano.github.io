KDrama: A RollerCoaster of Emotions.

September 18,
by Kate
in Korean Dramas
8 min read

I have been a fan of Korean Dramas since 3rd year high school and I watched a lot of series that made me laugh, cry, and even taught me lessons about life. I also admire these shows because of the story line, casts, and the amazing visual effects.Even though I have watched a lot of Kdramas, I have a list of my Top Ten dramas that made me love korean drama.

If you are looking for one of the best korean dramas, you are on the right place! Since I watch a lot of kdramas, I guarantee you that my recommendations will be worth the watch. I will introduce you my personal top ten korean dramas up-to-date and that you will surely adore. WARNING: may contain major spoilers XD.

When choosing what korean drama to watch, I always consider the casting especially when Song Joong Ki, So Ji Sub, Ji Chang Wook, Jung Hae In, and other oppas are in the drama. Next, I consider the theme, genre, or the main storyline of the drama if it will be interesting to watch. When the drama started, I will watch the first episode to know if I will be hooked or not. So without further ado, here are my top ten korean dramas to recommend for those wandering fans. NOTE: in random order.

10. Legend of the Blue Sea

If you want to watch a drama about mermaids like the story of little mermaid, you should watch the 'Legend of the Blue Sea'. Plus, the lead roles are: our childhood oppa Lee Min Ho and the forever gorgeous Jun Ji Hyun! You will surely fall in love with Jun Ji Hyun in this drama since she's so cute here :D. Well the main story is about a mermaid falling in love with a con-artist. There are also transitions between Joseon era and the modern era if you also like watching historical dramas.

9. Cinderella With Four Knights

This is not your typical cinderella story who finds his prince but this is a story of a girl who entangles her fate with four men (but not all the guys fall in love with her) But out of them, who will be her prince? If you are searching for oppas, I recommend you this drama since it does not have only one lead actor but four! Plus, you will like Park So-Dam here because she's so cute with that not so cinderella hairstyle.

8. Because This Is My First Life

If you want to watch a not-so-typical love story with full of sincerity (and sometimes weirdness) this is the drama for you. With Lee Min Ki and Jung So Min as leads and the adorable caaaat, this drama will make you feel the real feelings of people in their situation. Plus, there are also side stories about their friends where you could also learn about the reality of love and the life of adults. You will experience the different type of 'kilig' here even when they are only talking.

7. Cheese In The Trap

If you are a college student, you will surely relate to this drama since it revolves around the story of college students and a love story. If you are working, you will feel nostalgic since they will make you remember the life of being a college student and if you are a high school student, you will learn about the life when you get in college. Even though the main guy character is somehow strange, you will find their love story sweet (sometimes awkward). Plus, I love Lee Sung Kyung's crazy acting here haha.

6. Go Back Couple

One of my favorite dramas since I feel a lot of emotions. I cried, laugh and symphatize with the characters. It has the transition between the future and the past lives of the main characters where they time travel to get a chance to change their future. Will they choose their own happiness? or just change their feelings for each other? You will also learn about the different kinds of love here. Prepare also your tissues since the story involves family.

5. Descendants of the Sun

A love story about a doctor who saves lives and a soldier who took lives to save other people. This is the first ever drama that feels like a movie and has become a big hit not just in Korea but also in other countries. You will pay respect to soldiers and doctors after watching this drama and will surely make you fall-in-love more to Song Joong Ki and Song Hye Kyo. There are breath taking scenes that will make you more immersed in the story so brace yourselves.

4. The Last Empress

Another extraordinary drama where historical era is combined with the modern era. Imagine the empress wearing a traditional dress even today. This is also one of the most intense drama that I have watched with a lot of crazy scenes and unexpected ones. Full of betrayals to the point that you don't know who to believe. You will love Jang Na Ra more in this drama, you will be like "Yeah don't just watch, teach them a lesson".

3. Arthdal Chronicles

Song Joong Ki, Kim Ji Won, Jang Dong Gun, Kim Ok Bin. A Star-studded cast set in a different era unlike other dramas. You will also fall in love with the aesthetics of this drama and the storyline. A different side of Joong Ki will be shown and Kim Ji Won will leave you in awe with her acting. Such a great tandem for this year. Also, there are a lot of characters introduced so be ready with the different twists.

2. My Mister

A heart-warming drama that will also give comfort to those struggling with their lives. If you can relate to the main characters, this drama could also heal your lonely hearts. This is not a love story between a man and a woman but a love story between people who understand each other. I fell in love with Lee Sun Gyun Ahjussi more in this drama because he portrays the role like he is the real Park Dong Hoon. The main highlight is the immersive acting of Lee Ji Eun (IU) where the viewers praised her as a great actress. You will learn a lot in this drama especially about growing up and the problems and struggles you can face when you are in the real-world at the age of 40 to 50. All I can say is "You are all a good person".

1. Hotel Del Luna

You can feel the different kinds of love here and you will be immersed in the concept of the hotel. There are different stories that are relatable and there are the stories of the ghost. You will also be hooked in the story of the life of Jang Man Weol which is played by IU and how she became immortal and the owner of the hotel. I also liked the stories of the hotel staff and the love story between Man Weol and Goo Chan Seong ( Yeo Jin Goo ). Don't forget to bring some tissues because there are emotional scenes that will surely make you cry. Watch until you reach the ending because there will be a surprise.

Bonus: Goblin

One of the best dramas out there with amazing special effects. A cute love story between a Goblin and the Goblin's wife. There are also cute scenes between the Goblin and the Grim Reaper. If you watch the first episode, you cannot stop because of the introduction of the drama. I will not spoil any more information and I'll just say that this drama is a masterpiece so watch it before you get spoiled. Plus the OST is <3.
